Definition

What is Private Equity?

什么是私募基金？

Private equity funds are non-publicly offered investment vehicles targeting high-net-worth individuals and institutional investors. They offer high flexibility and return potential but carry elevated risks. With proper risk management, PE funds represent a premium wealth management tool.

私募基金是一种非公开募集的投资基金，主要面向高净值人群或机构投资者，具有较高的灵活性和潜在收益。有良好的风险管理，私募基金是一项优质的理财产品。

Non-Public Offering

非公开募集

Raised through private negotiation with qualified investors, without public advertising.

通过私下协商方式向特定合格投资者募集资金，不通过公开渠道宣传。

High Entry Threshold

投资门槛高

Investors must demonstrate significant capital and risk tolerance capacity.

通常要求投资者具备较高的风险承受能力和资金实力。

Flexible Oversight

监管相对宽松

Fewer disclosure requirements, allowing for more flexible investment strategies.

相比公募基金，信息披露要求较低，策略更灵活。

Classification
分类

Primary Types

主要类型

Private Equity (PE)

Investing in mature companies for expansion or buyouts.

私募股权基金，主要投资成长期或成熟期企业。

Venture Capital (VC)

Focusing on early-stage startups with high growth potential.

创业投资基金，侧重早中期初创企业。

Hedge Funds

Using long/short and arbitrage strategies to generate alpha.

对冲基金，通过多种策略获取超额收益。

Trade Funds

Quantitative and systematic trading in liquid markets.

交易基金，量化和系统化交易。

Features
特点

Key Characteristics

主要特点

High Return Potential

高收益潜力

Through leverage and niche strategies, PE funds can achieve above-market returns.

通过杠杆、长期持有或特殊策略获取超额收益。

Low Liquidity

低流动性

Lock-up periods of 1-5 years. Investors cannot redeem during this period.

锁定期通常1-5年，期间无法赎回。

Fee Structure

费用结构

Management fee (1-2%) + Carried interest (20% of profits).

管理费(1-2%) + 业绩提成(收益的20%)。

Confidentiality

信息保密

No public disclosure of holdings or strategies.

无需公开持仓，策略保密性强。

Risks & Challenges

风险与挑战

Important: PE funds are high-risk, high-reward instruments. Investors must fully understand the risks before committing capital.

重要提示：私募基金是高风险高收益的投资工具，投资者必须充分了解风险。

Market Risk

市场风险

Economic cycles and sector volatility impact returns.

经济周期、行业波动影响投资回报。

Liquidity Risk

流动性风险

Capital locked during investment period.

资金锁定周期长，紧急情况难以退出。

Managerial Risk

管理风险

Reliance on fund manager expertise.

依赖基金经理能力与职业道德。

Regulatory Risk

政策风险

Changing compliance landscapes.

监管变化可能限制运作模式。

如何选择私募基金？

Track Record

历史业绩

Review IRR, drawdowns, and Sharpe ratio.

回报率、回撤、夏普比率等指标。

Team Expertise

团队背景

Manager industry experience and success rate.

管理人行业经验、过往项目成功率。

Strategy Alignment

策略匹配

Match with your risk tolerance and goals.

是否与自身风险偏好、投资目标一致。

Compliance Status

合规性

Verify regulatory filings and history.

是否完成备案，是否存在违规记录。
