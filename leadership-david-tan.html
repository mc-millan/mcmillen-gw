President’s Message

Dato' Seri David Tan

拿督斯里陈泰圩

President of Mc Millen Group麦壳米伦集团总裁

The New Normal

Over the past few years, the global environment has continued to evolve. Geopolitical dynamics, economic cycles, and technological transformation have become increasingly interconnected, making uncertainty the new normal.

Against this backdrop, Mc Millen Group has remained committed to a fundamental belief: true and enduring value can only be built on long-term vision, stability, and authenticity.

Our Foundation

Since its establishment, the Mc Millen Group has been firmly rooted in the real economy, actively participating in infrastructure development, real estate projects, and a number of landmark initiatives. At the same time, we have continued to expand into green sustainability, arts, technology, and healthcare. While diverse in nature, these business segments share a common purpose — the creation of genuine, long-term value.

As the Group's operations continue to deepen, it has become increasingly clear to us that finance should not be detached from the real economy, and that capital should serve sustainable, long-term development. It is under this philosophy that the Group's financial business has evolved into a core strategic pillar, and further led to the establishment and development of Mc Millen World, designed with a more professional, transparent, and robust governance framework to support the Group's future financial strategy.

"Prudence, compliance, and responsibility must always take precedence over innovation."

Future Vision

Looking ahead, Mc Millen Group will continue to advance the deep integration of the real economy, capital markets, and digital finance. We believe that technology—particularly artificial intelligence—will continue to reshape financial efficiency and risk management. However, regardless of how technology evolves, prudence, compliance, and responsibility must always take precedence over innovation.

We do not pursue short-term trends, nor are we driven by market sentiment. Our long-term vision is to build an international platform with institutional governance, cross-regional collaboration, and sustainable growth capabilities across major global financial and economic hubs—enabling capital to flow, within a secure and transparent framework, towards opportunities that truly generate lasting value.

Looking forward, Mc Millen Group will continue to anchor itself in stability, build upon professionalism, leverage technology as a tool, and uphold responsibility as its foundation—steadfastly pursuing a path of long-termism in an ever-changing world.

时代的信念

过去数年，全球环境持续变化，地缘政治、经济周期与科技变革交织叠加，不确定性成为新常态。

在这样的时代背景下，Mc Millen Group 始终坚持一个信念：真正有价值的事业，必须建立在长期、稳健与真实之上。

实体根基与金融深度

自成立以来，集团以实体经济为根基，深度参与基础设施建设、房地产及多项标志性项目，并持续布局绿色环保、可持续发展、艺术、科技与医疗健康等领域。这些板块背后共同指向一个核心——《真实价值的创造》。

我们愈发清晰地认识到，金融不应脱离实体，资本更应服务长期发展。集团金融业务逐步成长为最具战略意义的核心板块，并进一步推动 Mc Millen World 的设立，以更专业、更透明的架构，承载未来的金融布局。

"科技将重塑金融的效率与风控方式；但无论技术如何演进，审慎、合规与责任感，始终应置于创新之前。"

长期主义愿景

展望未来，Mc Millen Group 将持续推进实体经济、资本市场与数字金融的深度融合。我们相信，科技，尤其是人工智能，将重塑金融的效率与风控方式；但无论技术如何演进，审慎、合规与责任感，始终应置于创新之前。

我们不会追逐短期风口，也不会被市场情绪左右。集团的长期愿景，是在全球主要金融与经济枢纽中，构建一个具备制度化治理、跨区域协同与可持续增长能力的国际化平台，让资本在安全、透明的体系中，流向真正具备长期价值的方向。

未来的 Mc Millen Group，将继续以稳健为本、以专业为基、以科技为工具、以责任为底线，在不断变化的世界中，坚定走在长期主义的道路上。
